Research in Critical-Section Granularity

Research

Studied how safety-critical and less-critical software that share resources can still meet strict timing guarantees in a mixed-criticality real-time system. We worked on a response-time model showing how the size and structure of critical sections trade off blocking, abort overhead, and timing guarantees, giving guidelines for choosing critical-section granularity.
